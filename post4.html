Why I think most devs should write more

November 2024  ·  5 min read

Writing is thinking. When you write about what you're building, you understand it better.

Writing forces clarity

If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it yet.

It compounds over time

Every post is a record of where you were. Looking back shows you how much you've grown.

It helps other people

That thing you figured out after hours of struggle? Someone else is struggling with it right now.
